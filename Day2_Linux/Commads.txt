Piping Concepts
tee command works as piping command which works like two directional pipe , vertical and horizontal like "T" 
date | cut -d' ' --field=1
date | tee  fulldate.txt | cut -d' ' --field=1 
date | tee  fulldate.txt | cut -d' ' --field=1 > today.txt  - It saves full date in fulldate.txt and outpur of cut command will be saved in today.txt
date | tee  fulldate.txt | cut -d' ' --field=1 | tee today.txt

xargs Commands
The xargs command allows you to convert piped data into command line arguments
date | xargs echo
Wed Aug 20 08:15:50 UTC 2025

date | xargs echo "hello"
hello Wed Aug 20 08:16:04 UTC 2025

date | cut -d' ' --field=1 | xargs echo
Wed

cat deletfiles.txt | xargs rm - this deletes the listed files in deletfiles.txt
